The Western Light Salt Lake City, Salt Lake, Utah (1914-1914)

The Western Light, edited in 1914 by James McAdams, was published "in the interests of the colored race," affirming "justice if possible, peace at any rate." This web site includes only one issue: Saturday, May 30, 1914. Utah Digital Newspapers
